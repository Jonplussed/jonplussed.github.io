Biography

Relevant to software, I have three passions: tooling, data, and the web— the web because of its unparalled ability to distribute information, and data because the proper analysis and presentation of allows people to make informed— and therefore better— decisions. Tooling— so contentious!— is more a matter or priority, and my priority is this: I desire to craft software that is correct, readable, elegant, and performant, and no language provides me this capability as does Haskell (and associated PureScript, Elm, etc)— the basis for my current and future endeavors.
